This is the story of an invention from its ideation to commercialization through the innovation ecosystem at the University of Oklahoma.

What if English was learned in China 1,000 years ago? What would it have looked like? How would it have evolved from the heights of the Song Dynasty, to the rise of England as a global superpower, to the present where American English is the language of global trade and commerce? One invention, to learn English through Chinese characters, would have solved very different problems depending on the historical context of each era.

Explore the transformations of a single invention over a span of 1,000 years and discover the relationship between technology and culture with Poetics of Invention.
